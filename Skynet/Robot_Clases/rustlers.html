Rustlers are basically scouts, however, they can and they do engage in combat. They can be weaponized based on mission needs and circunstances. Rustlers can perform scouting missions in any terrain. If needed, they will deploy propellers to go airborne if terreain geography is unmanagle in bipedal mode. They are powered by the well know Plutonium-239 nuclear battery, which means that a Rustler will complete any scout mission for long periods of time, even years. Always scouting before other military forces incrusions means data collected on the enemy moves and locations. This gives the advanatge of knowing where, what, when, how, how many, etc. ensuring a successful military incursion on enemy's land. Rustlers' AI interface capabilities gives them the capability to recongnize target enemies based on facial recognision and analize finger prints against its database.
