Fleshlinks are basically the workers, the servants. Fleshlinks application is aimed at manufacturing needs, home services needs, and other light to medium duty needs. This robots are well suite to provide the higehst service, they can perform task deemed dangerous for humans. They preprogram with basic servicibility modules, but it can be re-programed or retrianed to fit an individual and or very specific need such as elderly companionship and help, restaurant sanitation and servicing.
