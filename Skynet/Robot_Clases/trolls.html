Trolls are the biggest Robots ever built. It is constructed of Adamantium, the hardest and virtually undestructable material everknown known. Trolls has the capability of withstand hurricane cat 5 winds and beyond, by deploying its own anchoring devices that deepdive into earth as deep ass 6 feet. It is fire proof and if any substantial damage is detected, the robot will return to its home base for analysis and repair. The robot interface is basically AI, with a two key synchronized and assynchronized voice deactivation method, which will deactivate a Troll in case of an emergency. Trolls' application is for military purposes, its precense and architecture is to impose fear on its target and every Robot can be weaponized to suit the needs and circunstances. Trolls are powered by its a nuclear battery made out of "Plutoniun-239 -Fuel grade". No batteries to replace or recharge for more than 10 years, and Plutonium-239 fuel grade is no radioactive meaning that in the even a Troll is destroy in action, there is no radioactive potential to worry about, hence Plutonium-239 Fuel grade.
